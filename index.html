About Us

We are a student-run club at Dublin High School. Our mission is to help as many people as we can learn more about mathematics and how it applies to the real world. We will have interactive projects that can and WILL engage anyone who wants to learn more about math. We provide a calm and fun environment where students can share ideas and learn more about, as stated by the name, different mathematical tips, and tricks that can be applied to solve real-world problems.

General Info

  • The Math Tips and Tricks club is oriented around providing students with stratagem concerning tests and exams that they will further endeavor in their math academics. The club will also provide students with short-cuts catered for standardized tests such as the SAT, ACT, and PSAT in order to give our future Gaels a more probable chance of succeeding in their mathematical career. In addition to Tips and Tricks, we will give students a foundation on how to maintain accuracy while remaining within the time constraints of that specific test. With these newly developed skills, students will receive the basic understandings necessary to become successful mathematicians.

Our Purpose

  • We want every student on campus to learn while having fun with us in our club. Our goal is to improve everybody’s skills in math; regardless of which math course they are taking. We want our Gaels to learn new concepts and tricks to help them accelerate their ability to do the math that will assist them in higher classes. We also want to improve their logical thinking skills. Word problems can be hard to comprehend in some cases. We will teach students different ways to understand and simplify word problems. We will also be working on assignments collaboratively during the club.

What We Will Be Learning

    • Basic arithmetic tips
    • SAT tips
    • How to speed up calculations using various methods
    • Logical thinking
    • Ability to comprehend complex problems

Rules & Regulations

    • You must be present at the majority of the MTT club meeting
    • You should be actively participating in the club
    • Don’t be late to meetings
    • Respect everybody in the club. No put downs!!
    • Do not talk about irrelevant topics in the group chat
    • Have fun and get to know everybody! The more people you know, the more help you can get
